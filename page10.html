Why pay with PayPal

Pay faster and get your items sooner

Sellers usually only post to buyers once they get paid, so paying by cheque or postal order can delay delivery time. Pay instantly with PayPal and you can receive your items faster. You can even use the eBay app to pay for items with PayPal on your mobile to our PayPal Holding account.
